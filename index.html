Welcome!

Welcome to the digital display of items used in Apollo 11, which was the mission that sent the first humans to the moon, changing the course of human history. This site invites you to explore key elements of this extraordinary journey such as, the well-known spacesuit that Neil Armstrong wore when he took his first steps on the moon, the iconic spacecraft that sent the astronauts to the moon, and the complex interior that kept them alive. Come discover and learn about the unique features, materials used, functions and design innovations that enabled this historical achievement.
